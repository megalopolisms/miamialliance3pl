### 3.3 Technical SEO Gaps (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

1. **Missing Service schema on services.html** - Has BreadcrumbList but could benefit from expanded Service schema with individual serviceType entries
2. **No FAQ schema on FAQ page** - Competitors like Fulfillment Lab have FAQ schema for rich snippets
3. **Duplicate position numbers in CollectionPage schema** on blog.html - Multiple items share position 2, 3 which is invalid
4. **No LocalBusiness schema** - index.html has Organization but not LocalBusiness (critical for Google Maps/Local Pack)
5. **No review/rating schema** - Competitors display trust metrics; adding AggregateRating would help
6. **Image alt text audit needed** - Blog card images use gradient backgrounds with icon spans, no meaningful alt text
7. **No hreflang tags** - Site has Spanish translations but no hreflang link elements for proper multilingual SEO

### 3.4 Content Strategy Gaps

1. **No "vs" comparison articles** - Competitors benefit from "3PL vs 4PL", "in-house vs 3PL" comparison content
2. **No case study / customer success pages** - All competitors leverage social proof; Pablo Nicotine Pouches could be a case study
3. **No glossary/terminology page** - High search volume for basic terms
4. **No geographic service area pages** - Competitors have pages for Doral, Hialeah, Homestead; Miami Alliance serves these but has no dedicated landing pages

## 4. Three Priority Topics to Create

### Topic 1: "Complete Guide to 3PL Onboarding: What to Expect When Switching Fulfillment Partners"

- **Target Keywords:** 3PL onboarding, how to onboard with a 3PL, 3PL setup process, switching fulfillment providers, 3PL integration timeline
- **Rationale:** No competitor in the Miami market has a comprehensive onboarding guide. The Fulfillment Lab mentions onboarding timelines in FAQ (1-6 weeks) but has no dedicated article. This fills an information gap for decision-stage prospects who are comparing 3PLs and wondering what happens after they sign.
- **Search Volume Opportunity:** 2,000-4,000/month aggregate for cluster
- **Content Angle:** Step-by-step guide covering inventory audit, platform integration (Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce), SKU cataloging, test orders, go-live timeline. Include Miami Alliance's specific onboarding process.

### Topic 2: "Climate-Controlled & Cold Storage 3PL Warehousing in Miami: The Complete Guide"

- **Target Keywords:** climate controlled warehouse Miami, cold storage 3PL Florida, temperature controlled fulfillment, FDA warehouse Miami, supplement storage 3PL
- **Rationale:** Multiple specialized competitors (AMZ Prep, Neptune Cold Storage, Go Warehouse) dominate this niche. Miami Alliance already handles food and supplements but lacks a keyword-optimized authority page on climate-controlled capabilities. Miami's climate makes this especially relevant.
- **Search Volume Opportunity:** 1,500-3,000/month aggregate for cluster
- **Content Angle:** Types of temperature zones, FDA compliance requirements, industries served (food, supplements, beauty, pharma), cost comparison of climate-controlled vs standard storage, why Miami's heat makes climate control essential.

### Topic 3: "In-House Fulfillment vs. 3PL: Complete Cost Comparison for E-Commerce Brands (2026)"

- **Target Keywords:** in-house fulfillment vs 3PL, 3PL cost comparison, build vs outsource fulfillment, fulfillment cost calculator, when to outsource fulfillment
- **Rationale:** This is one of the highest-volume keyword clusters in the 3PL space (5,000-10,000 monthly searches). While Miami Alliance has a "when to switch" article, it doesn't directly address the cost comparison that prospects are searching for. Every major 3PL content strategy needs this cornerstone piece.
- **Search Volume Opportunity:** 5,000-10,000/month aggregate for cluster
- **Content Angle:** Side-by-side cost breakdown (rent, labor, software, shipping rates, insurance, equipment), hidden costs of in-house, break-even analysis by order volume, real-world scenarios at 100, 500, 1000, and 5000 orders/month.
