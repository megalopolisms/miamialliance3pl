# SEO Iteration: Brands, Factories, and Wholesale Intent

Date: 2026-03-27
Site: miamialliance3pl.com

## Goal

Increase qualified inbound interest from:

- Brands expanding from DTC into wholesale and retail
- Factories and manufacturers looking for a U.S. 3PL
- Buyers searching for wholesale-order fulfillment in Miami

## Audit Summary

- The site already had strong money pages:
  - `/3pl-for-brands.html`
  - `/3pl-for-factories.html`
  - `/wholesale-3pl.html`
- The brand page contained a broken internal resource link (`blog/choosing-3pl-miami-ecommerce-brand.html`).
- The homepage still skewed heavily toward gaming/electronics in its primary metadata and hero copy, which reduced direct match for broader factory/brand/wholesale intent.
- Support content for the target cluster was incomplete. There was wholesale content, but the transition-stage questions for brands and the manufacturer-specific evaluation questions were under-served.

## Implemented Changes

### 1. Homepage positioning

- Broadened the homepage title, description, OG/Twitter tags, hero copy, and FAQ coverage to include brands, factories, and wholesale fulfillment buyers.
- Kept gaming/electronics as differentiation rather than the only primary message.

### 2. Support content cluster

- Published `blog/dtc-to-wholesale-fulfillment-scaling.html`
  - Purpose: capture brand-scaling / DTC-to-wholesale intent
  - Funnel role: decision-stage bridge from `/3pl-for-brands.html` and `/wholesale-3pl.html`
- Published `blog/factory-3pl-manufacturer-wholesale-fulfillment-guide.html`
  - Purpose: capture factory/manufacturer 3PL intent
  - Funnel role: decision-stage bridge from `/3pl-for-factories.html` and `/wholesale-3pl.html`

### 3. Internal linking

- Fixed the broken brand-page resource link.
- Updated the resource modules on brand, factory, and wholesale landing pages to point toward the new support pages.
- Added the new articles to the homepage preview section and the blog hub.

### 4. Crawl support

- Added both new URLs to `sitemap.xml`.
- Refreshed homepage `lastmod`.
