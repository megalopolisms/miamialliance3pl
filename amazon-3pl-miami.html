Amazon 3PL Miami — Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Amazon sellers about our Miami prep center

What Amazon 3PL services do you offer?

Complete Amazon seller services: FBA prep (FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, bubble wrapping, bundling), FBM fulfillment, inventory storage at $0.75/pallet/day, inspection & QC, box content labeling, and returns processing.

How much does FBA prep cost?

FNSKU labeling: $0.20/unit. Poly bagging: $0.30/unit. Bubble wrapping: $0.50/unit. Bundling: $1.00/bundle. Box content labels: $3.00/box. Inspection: $0.15/unit. No minimums, no contracts.

Do you handle FBA and FBM?

Yes! We handle both FBA prep (preparing and shipping to Amazon warehouses) and FBM fulfillment (shipping directly to customers). Many clients use a hybrid approach for maximum flexibility.

Same-day processing. Inventory received before 2PM EST is prepped and ready to ship to Amazon fulfillment centers the same day. Rush processing available for urgent shipments.

Significantly. Amazon charges $0.87+/cu ft monthly with long-term surcharges up to $6.90/cu ft. We charge $0.75/pallet/day with zero surcharges. Most sellers save 40-60% on storage costs.
