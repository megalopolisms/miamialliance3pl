About Us

Your trusted logistics partner in the heart of Miami

Our Story

Miami Alliance 3PL was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, efficient logistics services that help businesses thrive. Based in Medley, FL, we serve as a strategic hub for companies looking to reach customers across the United States, Latin America, and beyond.


Our facility is strategically located near Port Miami, Miami International Airport, and major interstate highways, giving our clients unparalleled access to domestic and international shipping routes.

Our Mission

We believe logistics should be simple, transparent, and reliable. Our mission is to be an extension of your team, handling your warehousing and fulfillment needs so you can focus on what you do best: growing your business.


Every package we handle is treated with care and precision. We measure our success by your success.
