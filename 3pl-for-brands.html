Brand Growth Challenges We Solve Every Day

If any of these sound familiar, you need a 3PL partner that understands brands — not just boxes

❗ Outgrown your garage?

Your living room is full of inventory and your kitchen table is a packing station. It worked at 20 orders a week. At 200, it is breaking you. We give you a professional fulfillment center without the overhead of leasing your own warehouse. Move your inventory to Miami Alliance 3PL and get your home back.

❗ Spending more time packing than selling?

Every hour you spend taping boxes is an hour you are not spending on marketing, product development, or customer relationships. We handle the entire pick-pack-ship operation so you can focus on growing your brand. Our team processes orders within hours of receipt, not days.

❗ Getting chargebacks from retailers?

Retailers have strict labeling, routing, and delivery window requirements. Miss one and you get chargebacks that eat into your margins. We know retail compliance — EDI-ready shipping, proper case labeling, and on-time delivery to retailers like Target, Walmart, and independent buyers.

❗ Can't keep up with TikTok orders?

Your product went viral. Orders are flooding in faster than you can ship them. Late shipments mean bad reviews and lost repeat customers. We scale with your demand spikes — whether it is 100 orders or 10,000 in a single day. TikTok Shop integration means orders flow directly into our system.

❗ Inventory scattered across locations?

Some stock at home, some at a friend's garage, some at a storage unit. You never know exactly what you have or where it is. We consolidate everything into one warehouse with real-time inventory tracking. One dashboard, every SKU, accurate to the unit.

❗ Losing money on shipping rates?

Individual shipper rates are expensive. You are paying retail prices for every label. We pass along our negotiated carrier rates from UPS, USPS, FedEx, and DHL. Most brands save 15-30% on shipping costs from day one. Lower shipping costs mean better margins or more competitive free-shipping thresholds.

Brand Fulfillment — Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from brands evaluating a 3PL partner for the first time or switching from another provider

What makes Miami Alliance different from ShipBob or ShipMonk?

Personalized service with a dedicated account manager — not a ticket queue. No hidden fees, no surprise surcharges that appear on your first invoice. Deep expertise in gaming, electronics, and consumer goods categories. Bilingual team (English and Spanish) that understands both U.S. and Latin American markets. We treat your brand like our own, with custom SOP development for every client rather than forcing you into a cookie-cutter workflow.

Do you support branded packaging?

Yes. We support fully custom branded packaging including custom boxes, tissue paper, branded stickers, promotional inserts, thank-you cards, seasonal wrapping, and gift packaging. Your unboxing experience is part of your brand identity — we execute it with the same precision as the pick-pack-ship operation. Send us your materials or work with our team to source packaging that matches your brand guidelines and budget.

What platforms do you integrate with?

We integrate with Shopify, Amazon (FBA prep and FBM), WooCommerce, TikTok Shop, MercadoLibre, eBay, Walmart Marketplace, and more. Custom API integrations are available for proprietary platforms and enterprise ERPs. Orders sync in real time — no manual CSV uploads, no delays between the moment a customer clicks "buy" and the moment we start picking their order.

Is there a minimum order volume?

We have a $1,000 monthly minimum spend. Whether you ship 50 orders a month or 50,000, Miami Alliance 3PL scales with your brand. We work with growing brands and established brands doing millions in annual revenue. Our pricing is transparent and volume-based — you pay for what you use, and rates improve as your volume grows. No penalties for slow months, no long-term contracts.

Can you handle both DTC and wholesale?

Yes. We manage DTC (direct-to-consumer), wholesale, retail replenishment, and marketplace fulfillment from one unified inventory pool. No separate stock allocations, no duplicate SKU management, no channel conflicts. One warehouse, one inventory system, every channel — from single-unit Shopify orders to 500-unit wholesale pallets shipping to retail buyers.

How do I track my inventory and orders?

Through our real-time customer portal. You get a dashboard showing current inventory levels by SKU and warehouse location, inbound shipment tracking, order status with shipping tracking numbers, return processing status, and historical analytics. Set low-stock alerts, export reports, and make data-driven restocking decisions. Access it from any device, anytime — no phone calls needed to check on your stock.
