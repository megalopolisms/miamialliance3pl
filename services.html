Miami 3PL Services FAQ

Common questions about our Miami 3PL warehouse services

What makes Miami Alliance the best Miami 3PL?

Miami Alliance 3PL combines a strategic Medley, FL location (8 miles from MIA, minutes from Port Miami), 99.8% order accuracy, same-day fulfillment, no minimum orders, and transparent pricing. We're rated 4.9 stars by our clients and integrate with Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, and all major e-commerce platforms.

How much do Miami 3PL services cost?

Our Miami 3PL pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. Costs depend on storage volume (per pallet/shelf), fulfillment volume (per order), and any special services (kitting, FBA prep, custom packaging). Use our free instant quote calculator or contact us for a custom proposal.

Do you offer Amazon FBA prep as part of your Miami 3PL services?

Yes! Our Miami 3PL warehouse provides comprehensive Amazon seller services including FBA prep (FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, bundling), FBM fulfillment, and warehouse storage. Visit our Amazon 3PL Miami page for details.

Can you pick up or drop off inside PortMiami and Port Everglades?

Yes. Our TWIC-credentialed, TSA-vetted team supports secure pickup and drop-off inside both ports and aligns the move with warehouse receiving, staging, or outbound preparation from Medley.

Can your Miami 3PL handle international shipping?

Absolutely. Our Miami 3PL location is ideal for international logistics — we're the gateway to Latin America and the Caribbean. We handle import receiving, export preparation, and coordinate with customs brokers and freight forwarders for seamless cross-border operations.

Do you handle e-commerce returns and reverse logistics?

Yes. Our returns management process covers the full cycle: receiving returned items, quality inspection and grading, restocking sellable inventory, and disposing of damaged goods. We process returns within 24-48 hours and can trigger customer refunds through integrated platforms. Read our returns management guide for more details.

Can you fulfill FDA-regulated products like supplements or beauty items?

Yes. We handle supplements, beauty products, food items, and other regulated goods with lot tracking, expiration date management, and FIFO (first-in, first-out) inventory rotation. Our warehouse meets storage requirements for FDA-compliant products. Learn more in our regulated product fulfillment guide.

Do you specialize in gaming and electronics fulfillment?

Yes — we are the #1 fulfillment center for gaming and electronics brands in the U.S. We handle consoles (Nintendo, PlayStation, Xbox), video games, peripherals, and consumer electronics with ESD-safe protocols, serialized inventory tracking, climate-controlled storage, and 24/7 high-security zones. Our Miami location also serves as a Latin America gaming distribution hub.

How do you protect high-value electronics during fulfillment?

Every electronics order goes through our ESD-safe handling process: anti-static packaging, grounded workstations, and protective inserts for fragile items. We track each unit by serial number, provide photo documentation before shipping, and offer insured storage for high-value inventory. Our climate-controlled facility protects against temperature and humidity damage.
