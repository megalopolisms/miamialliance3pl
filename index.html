Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our 3PL fulfillment services

What is a 3PL warehouse?

A 3PL (third-party logistics) warehouse handles storage, order fulfillment, and shipping for e-commerce businesses. Instead of managing your own warehouse, you outsource logistics to experts like Miami Alliance 3PL, letting you focus on growing your business.

How fast can you ship my orders?

Orders received by 2PM EST ship the same day. From our Miami location, we reach 80% of U.S. customers within 2 business days via ground shipping. Express shipping options are also available for next-day delivery.

Do you integrate with Shopify and Amazon?

Yes! We integrate seamlessly with Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, eBay, and other major e-commerce platforms. Orders sync automatically, and tracking updates are pushed directly to your customers.

What are your minimum order requirements?

We have no minimum order requirements. Whether you ship 10 orders or 10,000 per month, we scale with your business. No long-term contracts required‚Äîyou pay only for what you use.

How much does 3PL fulfillment cost?

Pricing varies based on storage needs, order volume, and product dimensions. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Get a free instant quote using our online calculator or contact us for a custom proposal.

Can you handle international shipping?

Absolutely! Our Miami location is ideal for international shipping, especially to Latin America and the Caribbean. We're 8 miles from Miami International Airport and minutes from Port Miami for ocean freight.
