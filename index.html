Miami 3PL FAQ — Common Questions Answered

Common questions about our 3PL fulfillment services

What is a 3PL warehouse?

A 3PL (third-party logistics) warehouse handles storage, order fulfillment, and shipping for e-commerce businesses. Instead of managing your own warehouse, you outsource logistics to experts like Miami Alliance 3PL, letting you focus on growing your business.

How fast can you ship my orders?

Orders received by 2PM EST ship the same day. From our Miami location, we reach 80% of U.S. customers within 2 business days via ground shipping. Express shipping options are also available for next-day delivery.

Do you integrate with Shopify and Amazon?

Yes! We integrate seamlessly with Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, eBay, and other major e-commerce platforms. Orders sync automatically, and tracking updates are pushed directly to your customers.

What are your minimum requirements?

We have a $1,000 monthly minimum spend. There's no minimum order count — whether you ship 10 orders or 10,000 per month, we scale with your business. No long-term contracts required.

How much does 3PL fulfillment cost?

Pricing varies based on storage needs, order volume, and product dimensions. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Get a free instant quote using our online calculator or contact us for a custom proposal.

Can you handle international shipping?

Absolutely! Our Miami location is ideal for international shipping, especially to Latin America and the Caribbean. We're 8 miles from Miami International Airport and minutes from Port Miami for ocean freight.

Do you handle returns and reverse logistics?

Yes. We manage the full returns cycle: receiving returned items, quality inspection, restocking sellable inventory, and processing damaged goods. Our Miami location is also ideal for international returns from Latin America and the Caribbean.

Can you fulfill FDA-regulated products like supplements and beauty items?

Yes. We handle supplements, beauty products, food items, and other regulated goods with proper lot tracking, expiration date management, and FIFO inventory rotation. Our warehouse meets the storage requirements for FDA-compliant products.

How does a Miami 3PL compare to a West Coast fulfillment center?

Miami offers significant advantages: lower warehouse costs than LA or SF, direct access to Latin American markets, proximity to Port Miami and MIA for imports, and 2-day ground shipping to the entire East Coast and Southeast. Many brands are moving fulfillment from California to Miami to cut costs and expand into LATAM markets.

Do you offer Amazon FBA prep services?

Yes. We provide full Amazon FBA prep including FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, bundling, quality inspection, and shipment to Amazon fulfillment centers. We also support FBM (Fulfilled by Merchant) with same-day shipping for orders placed by 2PM EST.

Can you pick up or drop off inside PortMiami and Port Everglades?

Yes. Miami Alliance 3PL supports secure pickup and drop-off inside both ports with TWIC-credentialed, TSA-vetted access and fast handoff into warehouse receiving, staging, and outbound coordination.
