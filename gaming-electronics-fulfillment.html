Why Gaming Brands Choose Miami Alliance 3PL

Six capabilities that separate a gaming fulfillment center from a standard warehouse

01

ESD-Safe Handling & Anti-Static Protocols

Every gaming and electronics order is handled at grounded workstations with anti-static mats and wrist straps. We use ESD-safe packaging materials — anti-static bags, foam inserts, and protective wrapping — to eliminate electrostatic discharge risk. Our staff is trained on ESD-safe procedures and we perform regular compliance audits to ensure your GPUs, consoles, and motherboards arrive in perfect condition.

02

Serialized Inventory Tracking (Per-Unit Serial Numbers)

Every gaming console, GPU, and electronic device is scanned and logged by its unique serial number at receiving. We maintain a complete chain of custody from inbound to outbound — so you can trace any individual unit through our system. This is essential for warranty management, recall compliance, fraud prevention, and customer dispute resolution. Look up any serial number in real time through our customer portal.

03

Climate-Controlled & Humidity-Monitored Storage

Our warehouse maintains a temperature range of 65–78°F and 40–60% relative humidity year-round. Continuous monitoring systems alert our team to any deviation. This protects gaming consoles with lithium batteries, sensitive PC components, and sealed collector-edition games from heat damage, moisture, condensation, and degradation — problems that plague standard warehouses in humid climates like Florida.

04

24/7 High-Security Zones for High-Value Inventory

Gaming consoles, GPUs, and limited-edition collectibles are high-theft targets. Our dedicated security zones feature 24/7 camera surveillance, restricted access controls, individual unit tracking, and comprehensive insurance coverage. Every inbound and outbound shipment is photo-documented. Optional additional insurance is available for ultra-high-value inventory like rare sealed games and limited-run hardware.

05

Console, Game & Accessory Expertise

We understand the gaming industry. Our team knows the difference between a Nintendo Switch OLED and a Switch Lite, between a PS5 Standard and Digital Edition, between a collector's edition and a standard release. This expertise translates to fewer picking errors, correct packaging for each product type, and fulfillment that meets the expectations of gaming enthusiasts who notice every detail.

06

Latin America Gaming Distribution Hub

Miami is the gateway to Latin America and the Caribbean. Our Medley, FL warehouse sits 8 miles from Miami International Airport and minutes from Port Miami — making us the ideal export hub for gaming brands targeting LATAM markets. We handle export documentation, international shipping coordination, and customs broker partnerships across Central America, South America, and the Caribbean.

Our Gaming Fulfillment Process

Four steps designed specifically for high-value gaming and electronics products

1

Receiving with Serial Capture

Your gaming inventory arrives at our warehouse. Every unit is counted, inspected for damage, and scanned by serial number into our system. We photograph each shipment and update your portal in real time. Any discrepancies are flagged immediately.

2

Climate-Controlled Storage

Products are stored in our climate-controlled facility at 65–78°F with 40–60% humidity. High-value items go into dedicated security zones with restricted access and 24/7 surveillance. Your inventory is organized, tracked by serial number, and accessible through your portal.

3

ESD-Safe Pick & Pack

When an order comes in, our team picks the exact unit (verified by serial number scan) at a grounded ESD-safe workstation. Products are packed with anti-static materials, protective inserts, and custom packaging. Every order is photo-documented before sealing.

4

Insured Shipping & Tracking

Orders ship via UPS, FedEx, USPS, or DHL with full tracking and insurance. We integrate with Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, and all major platforms for automated label generation. Same-day shipping on orders received before 2PM EST. International exports available to Latin America and worldwide.

Gaming & Electronics Fulfillment FAQ

Common questions from gaming brands about our specialized fulfillment services

What makes Miami Alliance 3PL the #1 gaming fulfillment center?

We are purpose-built for gaming and electronics. That means ESD-safe handling with grounded workstations, serialized inventory tracking per unit, climate-controlled storage (65–78°F, 40–60% RH), 24/7 high-security zones, and a team trained specifically on gaming products. Our 99.8% accuracy rate and same-day processing are verified across thousands of gaming orders.

Do you handle Nintendo, PlayStation, and Xbox console fulfillment?

Yes. We fulfill all major consoles: Nintendo Switch (OLED, Lite, standard), PlayStation 5 (Standard, Digital), Xbox Series X and Series S, Steam Deck, and retro/handheld devices. Each console is serialized at receiving, stored in climate-controlled conditions, and shipped with ESD-safe protective packaging and insurance.

What ESD-safe protocols do you use for electronics?

Our full ESD protection includes grounded workstations with anti-static mats and wrist straps, anti-static packaging (bags, foam, bubble), humidity control between 40–60% RH, trained staff on ESD-safe procedures, and regular compliance audits. This protects GPUs, motherboards, consoles, and all sensitive electronics from static damage.

How does serialized inventory tracking work?

Every unit is scanned by serial number at receiving and tracked through our system from inbound to outbound. You can look up any unit in real time through our customer portal. This supports warranty management, recall compliance, fraud prevention, and customer dispute resolution.

Is your warehouse climate-controlled for electronics?

Yes. We maintain 65–78°F temperature and 40–60% relative humidity year-round with continuous monitoring and automated alerts. This protects lithium batteries, prevents moisture and condensation damage, and keeps sealed collector items in pristine condition.

Do you ship gaming products to Latin America?

Yes. Our Miami location is the #1 gateway for gaming distribution to Latin America and the Caribbean. We handle export documentation, international shipping, and customs coordination. Many gaming brands use our Miami warehouse as their dedicated LATAM distribution hub.

How do you secure high-value gaming inventory?

High-value items (consoles, GPUs, limited editions) are stored in dedicated security zones with 24/7 surveillance, restricted access, serial number tracking, and photo documentation. Comprehensive insurance is included, with optional additional coverage for ultra-high-value inventory.

What does gaming fulfillment cost?

Pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. It depends on storage volume, fulfillment volume, and specialized services (serialized tracking, ESD handling, insurance). No minimums, no long-term contracts. New customers get a $100 free storage credit. Use our instant quote calculator for exact pricing.

Can you handle Amazon FBA prep for gaming products?

Absolutely. We offer full Amazon FBA prep for gaming products: FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, bubble wrapping, bundling, inspection, and box content labeling. We also handle FBM fulfillment for Amazon sellers who ship directly to customers. Same-day processing, no minimums.

Do you handle gaming returns and reverse logistics?

Yes. Our returns management process handles the full cycle for gaming products: receiving, inspection and functional testing, serial number verification, restocking sellable inventory, and disposition of damaged goods. We process gaming returns within 24–48 hours with detailed condition reports.
