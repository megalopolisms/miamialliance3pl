Dropshipping Fulfillment Miami — Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from dropshipping businesses about our Miami fulfillment services

What is dropshipping fulfillment?

Dropshipping fulfillment means we store your products and ship them directly to your customers when they order from your online store. You never touch the product. We handle receiving, storage, picking, packing, and shipping — all from our Miami warehouse.

How much does dropshipping fulfillment cost?

Pick & pack from $1.50/order, standard shipping from $3.50/pkg, storage at $0.75/pallet/day. No minimums, no contracts. Custom packaging, branded inserts, and kitting available. Use our free online quote calculator for your exact pricing.

Do you integrate with Shopify?

Yes! We integrate with Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, TikTok Shop, eBay, Etsy, and BigCommerce. Orders sync automatically — when a customer buys, we ship. Inventory and tracking updates flow back to your store in real time.

Can you do blind/white-label shipping?

Absolutely. Our most popular dropshipping service. Your branded packing slips, custom packaging, branded tape, and marketing inserts. Your customer never sees our name. The package arrives looking like it came directly from you.

How fast do you process dropship orders?

Same-day processing. Orders received before 2PM EST are picked, packed, and shipped the same business day. We maintain 99.8% order accuracy with barcode verification on every package.

Where is your dropshipping warehouse?

8780 NW 100th ST, Medley, FL 33178 — in Miami-Dade County's logistics corridor. 8 miles from MIA airport, 15 minutes from Port Miami. Ground shipping reaches 80% of U.S. customers in 2 days.
